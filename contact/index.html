Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to your projects?

We welcome contributions of all kinds! Check out the CONTRIBUTING.md file in each repository for guidelines. You can help with code, documentation, bug reports, or feature suggestions.

Do you offer consulting or custom development?

Yes! We're available for consulting on React Native, Unity, web development, and game development projects. Reach out through LinkedIn or email for inquiries.

Are your projects free to use?

Absolutely! All our projects are open source under MIT or similar permissive licenses. You can use them freely in personal and commercial projects.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

For security issues, please email us directly instead of opening a public issue. We take security seriously and will respond promptly to any reports.
